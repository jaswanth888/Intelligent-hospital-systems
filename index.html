Winter Blues

What are Winter Blues?
Many of us feel extremely tired and low around the time winter begins. This feeling of sadness and depression is also called winter blues. Winter blues or Seasonal Affective Disorder (SAD) affects a large segment of the population. It is depression that occurs every year during a particular season and can affect your mood and well-being. It is a treatable condition, but it is important to notice its triggering factors and make suitable lifestyle changes.

Food Problems: Is It an Allergy or Intolerance?

Nov 11

Eating food is essential, as it is a nutrition source that keeps you healthy and energized. Sadly, you may find yourself getting ill after eating certain types of food. The instant reaction would be to think of allergies, but it may not be so. Your body will reject food items; it is food intolerance. There is no instant reaction when you are intolerant to some food products. You may be able to eat a small amount without any trouble. On the contrary, a food allergy may be more severe and even life threatening.
